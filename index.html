Rules :

In Blackjack, you play against the dealer. You receive 2 cards face up and the dealer's first card is face up and the rest are face down. The objective of the game is to get closer to 21 than the dealer without going over 21. If your total goes over 21, it is called a “bust” and the game is lost.

When you receive your first two cards, you may either “STAND” or “HIT”. When you “STAND” it means you feel you are close enough to 21 and no longer wish any additional cards. If you wish to receive another card, you "HIT". You may draw as many cards as you want until you are close to 21 or until you “bust.”

If you are closer to 21 than the dealer, you win. If your hand is less than the dealer's, you lose. If the dealer "busts" you win as well. But if you "bust", you lose.

Card values :

Jacks, Queens, Kings count as 10. An Ace may be played as a one or an 11. All other cards are played at face value.
